Project Overview

My third project is a multi-page website designed as a personal portfolio. This website consists of several main sections: Home, which provides a brief introduction about myself and the purpose of the portfolio; Skills, showcasing the technical and non-technical skills I have acquired; Education History, which outlines my academic journey along with significant achievements; and Contact, containing contact information such as email and social media links to make it easier for visitors to reach me. This project allowed me to further explore front-end web development, including navigation between pages and maintaining consistent design throughout the sections. Additionally, it gave me the opportunity to practice organizing content that reflects both professionalism and creativity.
